Benefits of Solving PYQs

  • Exam Pattern: Understand the format and structure of your exams
  • Important Topics: Identify frequently asked questions and important topics
  • Time Management: Practice solving questions within time limits
  • Confidence Building: Reduce exam anxiety through familiarization
  • Self Assessment: Evaluate your preparation level and identify weak areas

  • Solve papers under timed conditions

  • Practice regularly, not just before exams
  • Compare your answers with model solutions
  • Track your progress over time
