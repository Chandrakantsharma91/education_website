Video Upload Guidelines

Recommended Content
  • Educational tutorial videos
  • Problem-solving sessions
  • Concept explanations
  • Exam preparation videos
Prohibited Content
  • Copyrighted material
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
  • Irrelevant or misleading videos
  • Low-quality or unclear content
Quality Standards:
  • Ensure videos are educational and relevant to students
  • Use clear and descriptive titles
  • Provide accurate descriptions of video content
  • Verify video links are working and accessible
  • Prefer videos with good audio and video quality

Tips for Effective Videos

  • Clear Audio: Ensure good sound quality for better understanding
  • Visual Aids: Use diagrams, animations, and examples
  • Step-by-Step: Break down complex concepts into manageable steps
  • Practice Problems: Include solved examples and exercises
