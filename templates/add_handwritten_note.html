Handwritten Notes Upload Guidelines

Recommended Content
  • Neatly handwritten notes
  • Clear and readable handwriting
  • Well-organized content
  • Colorful diagrams and highlights
Prohibited Content
  • Illegible handwriting
  • Copyrighted material
  • Incomplete or poor quality scans
  • Personal information
Quality Standards:
  • Ensure handwriting is clear and readable
  • Provide accurate and helpful descriptions
  • Organize content logically with proper spacing
  • Use good quality scans or photos
  • Verify content is educational and appropriate

Tips for Effective Handwritten Notes

  • Clear Writing: Write neatly and legibly for better readability
  • Visual Appeal: Use colors, highlights, and diagrams to make notes attractive
  • Organization: Use headings, bullet points, and proper spacing
  • Quality Scans: Ensure high-quality scans or photos of your notes
