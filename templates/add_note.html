Notes Upload Guidelines

Recommended Content
  • Chapter-wise study materials
  • Exam preparation notes
  • Concept explanations
  • Practice problems with solutions
Prohibited Content
  • Copyrighted material
  • Incomplete or poor quality content
  • Irrelevant or misleading descriptions
  • Personal information
Quality Standards:
  • Ensure PDF files are clear and readable
  • Provide accurate and helpful descriptions
  • Organize content logically
  • Use appropriate file names
  • Verify content is educational and appropriate

Tips for Effective Notes

  • Clear Structure: Organize content with headings and subheadings
  • Visual Aids: Include diagrams, charts, and examples where helpful
  • Key Points: Highlight important concepts and formulas
  • Practice: Include solved examples and practice problems
